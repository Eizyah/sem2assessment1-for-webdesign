EIZYAH'S FAVORITE PORK SINIGANG RECIPE

Pork Sinigang - it is one of the most famous cuisine and a signature dish in the Philippines. It has a sour but flavoury taste unlike any other dish.

Ingredients for the pork sinigang:

  • 7-8 cups of water
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Pork ribs/belly
  • Sinigang mix Gabi
  • Beans
  • Okra(any amount)
  • Gabi
  • Kangkong
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to make the pork sinigang:

  1. First is to boil water, then during the water is still boiling, cut the tomatoes and onions into 8 parts(2 parts each tomato and onion).
  2. Then after the water is boiling, add the already cutted tomatoes and onions with the pork rib/belly and wait for the pork to be soft.
  3. When the pork is soft, add Gabi and wait a while till the water looks thick.
  4. If the water looks thick already, add the sampalok mix, salt, and pepper and wait for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the beans and the okra and wait for 5 minutes.
  6. Lastly, add the kangkong and the sinigang is finished.
